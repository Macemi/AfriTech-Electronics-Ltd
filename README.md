## Business Overview  
AfriTech Electronics Ltd. is a US-based consumer electronic company specializing in innovative offerings in smartphones, tablets, and wearable technology. With a growing team of over 200 employees and an annual revenue of $2 million (2022).  

AfriTech Electronics is committed to delivering high-quality, cutting-edge products.  

The company has earned a strong global reputation for its dedication to technological advancements and customer satisfaction. As it expands, AfriTech continues to focus on emerging trends and building trust with customers worldwide.

---

## Business Problem
- **Negative Social Media Buzz**: The company is experiencing a surge in negative social media conversations about its products and customer service. These conversations are damaging its brand image.  
- **Customer Complaints**: Complaints related to product defects, delays in customer support response, and billing issues have been rising, leading to a drop in customer satisfaction.  
- **Product Recalls**: Recent product recalls have received significant media attention, causing panic among customers and stakeholders.  
- **Competitive Pressure**: Rivals are gaining market share by exploiting AfriTech Electronics Ltd.’s reputation challenges.

---

## Objective  
Leverage PostgreSQL to clean, structure, and analyze customer demographics, transaction patterns, and social media engagement. The goal is to uncover actionable insights that strengthen brand reputation, optimize marketing strategies, and support data-driven business decisions.  
Finally, develop a **4-page interactive dashboard** to visually communicate insights across key business areas.

## Data Preparation  
AfriTech Electronics Ltd. Dataset is a Microsoft Excel dataset containing multiple columns. The dataset is thoroughly cleaned—free of errors, duplicates, white spaces, and null values—ensuring it is reliable, original, comprehensive, up-to-date, and well-cited.  

The cleaned data was then imported into **PostgreSQL**, where structured queries were performed to extract meaningful insights and analyze the most relevant information for our objectives.  

After the transformation, the data was loaded into **Power BI**. Data models were verified to ensure accuracy and consistency before developing insightful and interactive dashboards. These visualizations enable effective, data-driven decision-making.

## Analysis  
- **Moderate-sized customer base** with significant spending power.  
- **Customer base is mature**, with an average age of 44.  
- **Broad regional coverage** suggests national reach.  
- **Older age groups contribute more income** on average.  
- **Younger age groups** show slightly lower income levels.  
- **Illinois has the wealthiest customer base.**  
- **Returning customers spend the most.**  
- **Laptops dominate sales volume.**  
- **Seasonal spikes** may align with back-to-school and holiday shopping.

- **Engagement rate is moderately healthy.**  
- **Balanced post engagement** across content types.  
- **Visual content (images, videos)** performs slightly better.

## Recommendations  

### Customer Demographics & Targeting  
- Focus marketing on **older age groups (60+)** who have the highest average income.  
- Build **retention and loyalty programs** for returning customers, the top earners among customer types.  
- Maintain **age-diverse campaigns** to keep engagement across all segments.  

### Regional Strategy  
- Allocate more resources to **high-income regions** like Illinois, Montana, and Connecticut.  
- Boost market penetration in **high purchase regions** like West Virginia and Nebraska, even if income is lower.  
- Customize campaigns **regionally** to fit demographic profiles.  

### Product Strategy  
- **Prioritize laptop inventory and promotions**, which dominate sales ($24.58M).  
- **Align product marketing with seasonal trends** — peak sales in June and October.  
- Maintain a **diverse pricing range** to serve both budget and premium buyers.  

### Engagement & Social Media  
- Focus on **TikTok and Instagram** for highest ROI on social media engagement.  
- **Leverage visual content (images & videos)** — slightly higher engagement rates.  
- **Improve positive sentiment** through community outreach and responsiveness.  

### Recall & Product Quality  
- Strengthen **product quality assurance** to reduce true recalls (~50% of total).  
- Develop a **faster, more transparent recall response system** to maintain trust.  

### Brand & Sentiment Positioning  
- **Emphasize brand uniqueness** to outshine competitors (currently equal mentions).  
- **Highlight positive sentiment growth** (up from 2022 to 2023) in branding.
